---
category: Builder
question: Can I open an existing poster setup in the builder?
order: 1
---

Yes. Two ways.

First: the examples gallery has an "Open in Builder" button on every preset. Click it, and your browser loads that exact layout in the editor.

Second: you can pass query parameters to `/build` directly. The examples gallery shows the raw query strings — copy one, append it to `/build?`, and you're in. Handy for bookmarks or sharing setups.
