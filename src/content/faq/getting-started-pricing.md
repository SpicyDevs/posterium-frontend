---
category: Getting Started
question: Is Posterium free to use?
order: 2
---

Yes. Completely free. MIT open source.

No pricing tiers, no credit counter, no "sign up for a trial" nonsense. The API is open — hit it as much as you want. The builder runs in your browser. If you're self-hosting, the worker code is right there in the repo.

There's no catch. We built this because we wanted it to exist.
